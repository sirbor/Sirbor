title: Kitchen
---

A modern Android study project that explores the intersection of social recipe discovery and on-demand delivery logistics. It seamlessly merges the culinary community spirit of Cookpad with the hyper-local delivery efficiency of Glovo, demonstrating a professional-grade implementation of a multi-module e-commerce platform.

### Tech Stack
- **Language:** Kotlin
- **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
- **Architecture:** MVVM + Clean Architecture
- **Navigation:** Compose Navigation with deep-link support
- **Reactive Logic:** Kotlin Coroutines & Flow
- **APIs:** Google Maps SDK (via maps-compose)
