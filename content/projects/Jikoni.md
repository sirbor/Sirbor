---
title: Jikoni
---

A high-fidelity iOS application designed to bridge the gap between home-cooked inspiration and local luxury commerce. It serves as a premium food economy platform where users can discover global recipes, order artisanal ingredients, and track deliveries through upscale districts.

### Tech Stack
- **Language:** Swift 5.10
- **Framework:** SwiftUI (Observation Framework)
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture (Domain, Data, Presentation)
- **Maps:** MapKit with realistic route simulation logic
- **Project Management:** XcodeGen
