---
title: Dukani
---

A domain-driven e-commerce framework for Django, tailored for high-end retail environments. Built upon the robust foundation of Oscar, it is designed for extensibility and precision, handling everything from large-scale B2C storefronts to complex B2B business logic with a flexible product catalog and extensible checkout.

### Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Django (4.2 - 5.2)
- **Language:** Python (3.8+)
- **Frontend:** Bootstrap 4, SCSS, jQuery
- **Search:** Django Haystack (Elasticsearch, Solr, Whoosh)
- **Database Utilities:** django-treebeard, django-tables2
- **Localization:** Babel (multi-currency, multi-language)
